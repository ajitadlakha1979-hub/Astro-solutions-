The Practitioner

Rooted in tradition,
present to your life

I've spent over a decade in the libraries and lineages of Western esotericism — studying under astrologers trained in the Hellenistic tradition, working through the Rider-Waite-Smith corpus, and immersing myself in Jungian depth psychology as a companion lens.

My practice is not fortune-telling. It is a structured method for self-knowledge — using ancient symbolic systems to illuminate patterns, tendencies, and possibilities that are already present in your life, waiting to be named.

I work with clients across India and internationally, holding space for the full complexity of a human life. Sessions are confidential, non-dogmatic, and grounded in genuine care.
